Research

I research how large language models (LLMs) can be enhanced to better handle figurative language and abstract concepts. In particular, I explore how diffusion models can be used to improve both the generation and interpretation of such language, aiming to strengthen the reasoning and expressive capabilities of LLMs. These improvements can support the development of better preforming smaller language models including lower-resource languages.

Topics of Interest

  • Computational semantics
  • Figurative language
  • Difussion Models
  • Multimodal models
  • Low-resource languages
