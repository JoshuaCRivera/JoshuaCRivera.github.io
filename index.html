About Me

I’m a first-year PhD student at IMT Atlantique in Brest, France, working with the BRAIn: Better Representations for Artificial Intelligence team in the Mathematical and Electrical Engineering (MEE) department. My research interests are revolve diffusion-based large language models(LLMs) and few-shot learning, specifically when applied to figurative language, low resource languages, and semantics.

Prior to starting my PhD, I completed my Master’s in Computational Linguistics at the University of Stuttgart in Germany and my Bachelor's in Linguistics and Mathematics at New York University(NYU) in the United States. I also previously held research positions at TU Darmstadt's UKP Lab and Cornell University’s Weill Cornell Medicine.
